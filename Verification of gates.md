# VERIFICATION OF GATES

## AIM

To study and verify the truth table of logic gates.

---

## APPARATUS REQUIRED

All the basic logic gate ICs mentioned below:

- IC 7404LS (NOT Gate)
- IC 7408LS (AND Gate)
- IC 7432LS (OR Gate)
- IC 7400LS (NAND Gate)
- IC 7402LS (NOR Gate)
- IC 7486LS (EX-OR Gate)
- IC Trainer Kit
- Connecting Wires
- Digital Multimeter

---

## PROCEDURE

1. Place the IC on the IC Trainer Kit.
2. Connect VCC and Ground to the respective pins of the IC Trainer Kit.
3. Connect the inputs to the input switches provided on the IC Trainer Kit.
4. Connect the outputs to the output LED indicators.
5. Apply various combinations of inputs according to the truth table and observe the condition of the LEDs.
6. Disconnect the output from the LEDs and note the corresponding multimeter voltage readings for various combinations of inputs.
